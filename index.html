Immersed in an artificial world, our bodies have physiologically adapted to withstand stress. We strongly believe that nature is wise and provides us with everything we need to live well.

They are ancient beings, plants and fungi, that adapted to hostile environments and extreme, adverse temperatures. For this reason, in the same way they adapted over thousands of years to different ecosystems, they developed regulatory (adaptogenic) properties for humans.

They help us reduce our body's exaggerated response to stress in these modern times with more technology, social media, work stress, the need for immediacy, and so on.

Our magic mushrooms

Lion´s mane: It increases cognitive capacity, such as memory and the process of learning. These effects are related to its neuroprotective properties and its ability to modulate brain plasticity.
Reishi: It is called the mushroom of immortality because of its wide-ranging benefits. Specific applications include modulating the immune system, hepatoprotection, bacteriostasis, and more.
Cordyceps: Its effectiveness is particularly evident in improving physical and sexual energy, as well as mood and humor. It is frequently used by athletes or individuals with high energy demands.
Lion´s mane treatment: In our triple pure extract spans six months to ensure that the results are long-lasting. Although the primary effects are often noticed within a few weeks, discontinuing the treatment before completing the cycle can result in the loss of achieved progress.
Reishi treatment: Its application can vary from an intensive treatment of a few weeks to a long-term treatment of 90 to 120 consecutive days. The dosage is adjusted according to the need and can be combined with other extracts depending on the situation to be resolved.
Cordyceps treatment: Maintaining serenity and normalizing essential processes are key characteristics of this adaptogen, making it a frequent companion in various treatments. It is recommended to consume doses of 2 to 6 ml daily. It can be part of 60-day treatments or consumed continuously without breaks.
